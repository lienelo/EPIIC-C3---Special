Core activities

Three activity pillars

SPECIAL aligns around three general activity types that can be adapted institution-by-institution: partnership management, team training, and collaboration events.

A modern, accessible layout for partners, faculty, students, and community stakeholders.

Partnership management

Create sustainable infrastructure—dedicated roles, coordination, and relationship-management systems (e.g., CRM).

  • Centralize partnership knowledge
  • Make follow-up consistent
  • Scale engagement beyond individuals

Team training

Externships, faculty fellows, and professional development to close skills gaps and align education with industry needs.

  • Faculty externships / immersion
  • Faculty fellows programs
  • Best-practice training

Collaboration events

Advisory boards, workshops, conferences, and showcases that connect faculty, students, and partners.

  • Industry advisory boards
  • Workforce development expos
  • Partner/college showcases
