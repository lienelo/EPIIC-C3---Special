3-year view

Timeline at a glance

Year 1 establishes foundations, Year 2 expands programs and engagement, and Year 3 formalizes sustainability and scaling.

Year Theme Typical actions across the cohort
Year 1 Foundation & partnership building Establish roles and operating cadence • Identify partners • Launch early externships/immersions • Create advisory structures • Begin CRM planning or adoption.
Year 2 Expansion & engagement Grow participation • Implement more partner opportunities (internships/projects) • Host additional events • Strengthen feedback loops through boards and surveys.
Year 3 Sustainability & scaling Formalize long-term partnership models • Expand programs into additional sectors • Embed operations into institutional budgets/policies • Share results and best practices.

Measurement

Track partners, projects, participants, events, and feedback to enable real-time adjustment during the award period.

Knowledge sharing
