About the consortium

A diverse cohort, one shared vision

SPECIAL is built on the idea that institutional diversity is an advantage: complementary strengths create more pathways to scalable, sustainable industry engagement.

A modern, accessible layout for partners, faculty, students, and community stakeholders.

Shared cohort goals

Expand external partnerships • Strengthen internal infrastructure • Enhance community and industry engagement.

Cohort-wide activities

Quarterly virtual meetings, annual in-person meetings, ad hoc working groups, and a shared real-time resource platform.

What success looks like

More partners, more collaborative projects, more participants and events—plus participant feedback that supports continuous improvement.
